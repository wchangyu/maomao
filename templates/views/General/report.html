Report introduction

The overall energy consumption and energy efficiency of the specific report response system include the following contents:


1、Energy efficiency data of cold station.

1.1、Overall situation.
1.2、Sharing situation.

2、Energy consumption data of cold station.

3、Data quality of cold station.

The electricity consumption report is the application of electricity consumption meter reading, including the following contents:


1、Calculation of electricity cost for cold station.

2、Power consumption statistics of cold station equipment.

3、Electricity consumption statistics of single equipment in cold station.

The load report shows the distribution of cold load and analyzes the demand:


1、Calculation of overall load of cold station.

2、The load statistics of cold stations show the relationship between load weights and corresponding energy efficiency.

3、The cold load distribution and the cooling curve in the display period.

The energy efficiency statement shows the calculation value of the cooling station energy efficiency, including the following contents:


1、Energy efficiency date, macro display of energy efficiency distribution.

2、Total sub energy efficiency analysis, showing energy efficiency calculation data of the whole system and equipment sub item.
