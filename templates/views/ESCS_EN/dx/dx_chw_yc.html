Individual or Integrated Pump EER can be calculated by below formula:

Pump Head = (Discharge Pressure – Suction Pressure) * 100 + Pump’s Height Differential
The unit of pressure is MPA
Normally, within the design range of water flow, the ideal circuit pressure drop shall be around 30 meters, once exceeds it, means increase pump operating power consumption.
Investigation
1. Strainer is blocked because dirty, high pressure drop cross over
2. Chiller evaporator tube is blocked because dirty
3. Existing valve position is wrong, only keep half or minimum opening
4. Because select big pump, then have to throttle the valve to prevent pump over current.
Recommendation
1. To clean and/or replace existing strainer
2. To check and clean for chiller evaporator tube
3. To check valve’s position and condition
4. To replace new pump, or to trim impeller
