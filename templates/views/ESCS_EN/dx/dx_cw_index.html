Definition -

Condenser Water Circuit Energy Efficiency Ratio = Cooling Capacity / Condenser Water Pump Power Consumption


Condenser water circuit EER indicate the amount of cooling delivery by one unit of condenser water pump power consumption. The figure is bigger, the better. Typical and Annual operation conditions can refer to the contents inside national standards.
